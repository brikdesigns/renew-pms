## Supply categories

Renew organizes supplies by category to simplify inventory management and task assignment. Default categories include:

| Category | Department |
|----------|-----------|
| Instruments | Clinical |
| PPE (Gloves, Masks, Eyewear) | Clinical |
| Disposables | Clinical |
| Autoclave Bags | Clinical |
| Cleaning Solutions | Clinical |
| Lab Materials | Clinical |
| Office Supplies | Clinical |

## Customizing categories

Practice admins can rename or add supply categories from **Settings**. Tasks related to supply management (restocking, inventory checks) can be linked to specific supply categories.

## Supply-related tasks

When creating a task, you can link it to a supply category. This helps track which tasks are related to inventory management — useful for daily opening/closing checklists and periodic inventory audits.
