title: Equipment Tracking
description: Managing equipment records, status, and maintenance schedules.
---

## Equipment records

Each piece of equipment in your practice has a record in Renew with:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-----------|
| **Name** | What it is (e.g., "Operatory 1 Chair") |
| **Category** | Dental Chair, Autoclave, X-Ray Machine, etc. |
| **Manufacturer / Model** | Brand and model number |
| **Serial number** | For warranty and service tracking |
| **Room** | Where it's located |
| **Status** | Active, Needs Service, or Out of Service |

## Equipment status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Active** | In normal use |
| **Needs Service** | Flagged for maintenance — still usable but attention needed |
| **Out of Service** | Offline — do not use until repaired |

## Maintenance tracking

Each equipment record tracks:
- **Last maintained** — when it was last serviced
- **Next maintenance due** — upcoming service date
- **Warranty expiry** — when the warranty ends
- **Compliance notes** — any regulatory notes

When maintenance is due, related tasks can be created automatically.
