## Practice Information

Update your practice name, address, phone, and other details from **Settings > Organization**.

## Offices

An office is a physical location (building). Most practices have one, but Renew supports multiple offices if your practice grows.

- View and edit your office details (address, phone, email)
- The **primary office** is marked with a star

## Rooms

Rooms are the spaces within your office — operatories, sterilization rooms, the break room, etc. Renew comes pre-configured with common dental rooms.

### Default rooms

Lobby, Front Office, Waiting Area, Operatory, Sterilization Room, X-Ray Room, Lab, Consultation Room, Supply/Storage, Break Room, Restroom

### Adding custom rooms

1. Go to **Settings > Organization**
2. Scroll to the **Rooms** section
3. Click **Add Room**
4. Enter a name and save

Custom rooms appear alongside the defaults and can be assigned to tasks and equipment.
