## Inviting a new team member

1. Go to **Settings > Users**
2. Click **Invite User**
3. Enter their email, first name, and last name
4. Select their **role** (e.g., Dental Hygienist, Receptionist)
5. Click **Send Invite**

They'll receive an email to set their password and access Renew.

## User status

Each team member has a status that reflects where they are in onboarding:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **New** | Recently invited — still getting set up |
| **Maturing** | Progressing through training |
| **Active** | Fully onboarded and ramped |

Status updates automatically based on training progress.

## Deactivating a user

If someone leaves the practice, deactivate their account rather than deleting it — this preserves their task history and audit trail.

1. Go to **Settings > Users**
2. Find the user
3. Click **Deactivate**
