## What are roles?

Roles represent job functions in your practice — what someone **is** (e.g., Dental Hygienist, Office Manager). They're separate from permissions, which control what someone **can do**.

## Default roles

Renew comes with common dental practice roles:

| Role | Department |
|------|-----------|
| Owner | Clinical |
| Office Manager | Administration |
| Dental Hygienist | Clinical |
| Dental Assistant | Clinical |
| Receptionist | Front Desk |
| Treatment Coordinator | Front Desk |
| Insurance Coordinator | Front Desk |
| Inventory Manager | Engineering |

## Customizing roles

Practice admins can rename existing roles or add new ones:

1. Go to **Settings > Roles**
2. Click a role to rename it, or click **Add Role**
3. Assign a department
4. Save

Roles determine which tasks and workflows are relevant to each team member.
