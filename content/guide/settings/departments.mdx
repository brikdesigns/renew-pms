## What are departments?

Departments group roles and tasks by area of the practice. Tasks can be assigned to an entire department rather than an individual.

## Default departments

| Department | Covers |
|-----------|--------|
| Clinical | Dentists, hygienists, assistants |
| Front Desk | Receptionists, coordinators |
| Engineering | Equipment, inventory management |
| HR | Hiring, onboarding |
| Administration | Office management |
| Sterilization | Instrument processing |
| Global | Applies to everyone |

## Customizing departments

1. Go to **Settings > Departments**
2. Rename, reorder, or add new departments
3. Assign a color for visual tagging (optional)
