## Training modules

Training modules are learning units assigned to your team. They can be practice-specific or global (shared across all practices).

### Module details

| Field | Description |
|-------|-----------|
| **Title** | Module name |
| **Category** | Topic area |
| **Difficulty** | Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced |
| **Estimated time** | How long it typically takes |
| **Required** | Whether completion is mandatory |

### Trainual integration

If your practice uses Trainual, training content is managed there — Renew syncs your assignments and completion status automatically. You'll see your Trainual training alongside any Renew-native modules.
