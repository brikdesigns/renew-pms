## Your training progress

View your training status from the dashboard or from the Training section. Each module shows:

| Field | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Not Started** | Assigned but not yet begun |
| **In Progress** | You've started but haven't finished |
| **Completed** | Finished successfully |
| **Failed** | Did not meet passing criteria — retry available |

## How training affects your status

Your training progress directly impacts your employee status:

- **New** — you've just joined and have minimal training completed
- **Maturing** — you're actively working through your required assignments
- **Active** — all required training is complete, you're fully ramped

This status determines which tasks and workflows are surfaced to you — new employees see onboarding tasks, while active employees see the full workload.
