## Editing your profile

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Go to **Account**
3. Update your first name, last name, phone, or avatar
4. Click **Save**

## What you can change

| Field | Editable? |
|-------|-----------|
| First name | Yes |
| Last name | Yes |
| Phone | Yes |
| Avatar photo | Yes |
| Email | No — contact your admin to update |
| Role | No — set by your practice admin |
