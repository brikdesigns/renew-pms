## Logging in

You'll receive an email invitation from your practice administrator. Click the link to set your password and access Renew.

1. Open the invitation email
2. Click **Set Password**
3. Choose a strong password
4. You'll be redirected to the Renew dashboard

## Trouble logging in?

- **Didn't receive the email?** Check your spam folder, or ask your office manager to resend the invitation.
- **Forgot your password?** Click **Forgot Password** on the login page to receive a reset link.
- **Account locked?** Contact your practice administrator.
