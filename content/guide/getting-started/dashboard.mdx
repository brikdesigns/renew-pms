## The Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard. This is your home base — it shows a summary of your tasks, schedule, and any items that need attention.

## Navigation

The sidebar on the left gives you quick access to all areas of Renew:

| Section | What it's for |
|---------|--------------|
| **Dashboard** | Your daily overview |
| **Tasks** | View and manage assigned tasks |
| **Schedule** | Your shift and task calendar |
| **Documents** | Practice files and SOPs |
| **Settings** | Account, organization, and team management |

## What you see depends on your role

- **Practice Admins** see all staff, settings, and management tools
- **Staff members** see their own tasks, schedule, and profile
