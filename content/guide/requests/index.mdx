## Requests

Requests are how your team reports issues with equipment, devices, or the facility itself. Any staff member can submit a request, and practice admins triage and resolve them.

- [Submitting a Request](/guide/requests/submitting) — how to report an issue
- [Tracking Requests](/guide/requests/tracking) — following progress and getting updates

## How it works

1. A staff member submits a request describing the issue
2. Practice admins are notified by email and in-app notification
3. An admin reviews the request and updates its status as work progresses
4. The submitter is notified when the status changes
5. Once resolved, the admin adds resolution notes and closes the request

## Request categories

| Category | Use for |
|----------|---------|
| **Device Issue** | Computers, tablets, monitors, printers |
| **Equipment Issue** | Dental chairs, autoclaves, X-ray machines, handpieces |
| **Facility Maintenance** | Building, plumbing, HVAC, electrical, general repairs |
