## Integrations

Renew PMS integrates with external services for training and document management. API keys are stored per-practice in `practices.integrations` (server-side only).

- [Trainual](/docs/integrations/trainual) — staff training sync, people management, and employee lifecycle
- [Google Drive](/docs/integrations/google-drive) — document source of truth for SOPs and practice files
